# Cymbal Retail — Returns & Refunds Policy (v4.2, Effective Jan 2026)

## 1. Standard Return Window
- Customers may return most items within **30 days of delivery** for a full refund.
- Items must be unused, in original packaging, with all tags attached.
- Proof of purchase (order ID or receipt) is required.

## 2. Extended Windows
- **Cymbal Plus members**: 60-day return window on all eligible items.
- **Holiday purchases** (Nov 1 – Dec 24): returnable until Jan 31 of the following year.

## 3. Non-Returnable Items
- Gift cards, downloadable software, and clearance items marked "Final Sale".
- Perishable goods and personal care items that have been opened.
- Custom or personalized products.

## 4. Refund Methods & Timelines
- Refunds are issued to the **original payment method**.
- Credit/debit card refunds: 5–7 business days after the warehouse receives the item.
- UPI / net-banking refunds: 3–5 business days.
- Cash-on-delivery orders: refunded as Cymbal store credit or bank transfer (customer's choice).

## 5. Return Shipping
- Free return shipping for defective, damaged, or incorrect items.
- For change-of-mind returns, a flat ₹99 / $4.99 return shipping fee is deducted from the refund.
- Cymbal Plus members always get free return shipping.

## 6. Damaged or Defective Items
- Report damaged items within **7 days of delivery** with photos.
- Customers may choose a replacement, repair, or full refund including original shipping.
- No return shipping fee applies.

## 7. Exchanges
- Size/color exchanges are processed as a return + new order to guarantee stock.
- The new order ships as soon as the return carrier scan is confirmed (no need to wait for warehouse receipt).

## 8. Escalation Rules for Support Agents
- Refunds above ₹20,000 / $250 require supervisor approval — create an escalation ticket.
- Suspected return fraud (more than 5 returns in 30 days) must be escalated to the Trust & Safety queue.
