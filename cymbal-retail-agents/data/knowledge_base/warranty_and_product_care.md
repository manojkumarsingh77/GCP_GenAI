# Cymbal Retail — Warranty & Product Care Guide (v2.5, Effective Feb 2026)

## Standard Warranty Coverage
- **Electronics (Cymbal-branded)**: 24-month manufacturer warranty from delivery date.
- **Electronics (third-party brands)**: warranty per the brand; Cymbal facilitates claims
  for the first 12 months.
- **Furniture**: 12-month warranty against manufacturing defects.
- **Apparel & footwear**: 90-day defect warranty (stitching, soles, zippers, hardware).
- **Small appliances**: 12 months, extendable to 36 months with Cymbal Care+.

## What Warranty Covers
- Manufacturing defects in materials or workmanship.
- Electrical or mechanical failure under normal use.
- Battery capacity dropping below 70% within the first 12 months (electronics).

## What Warranty Does NOT Cover
- Accidental damage (drops, spills, cracked screens) unless Cymbal Care+ is purchased.
- Normal wear and tear, cosmetic scratches.
- Damage from unauthorized repairs or modifications.
- Commercial use of consumer-grade products.

## Cymbal Care+ (Extended Protection Plan)
- Adds accidental damage protection with 2 claims per year.
- Extends warranty to 36 months on electronics and appliances.
- Priced at 10% of product price; purchasable within 30 days of delivery.
- Screen-only repairs have a ₹499 / $19 service fee; all other claims are free.

## Warranty Claim Process
1. Customer contacts support with order ID and issue description.
2. Support runs remote diagnostics (electronics) or requests photos (other categories).
3. Approved claims get a free pickup label; turnaround is 7–10 business days.
4. If the same defect recurs twice, the third claim is an automatic replacement
   (the "three-strike replacement rule").

## Escalation Rules for Support Agents
- Warranty disputes where the customer disagrees with a rejection: escalate to
  the Warranty Review Board queue with photos and diagnostic logs attached.
- Safety-related defects (overheating, smoke, sparks, sharp edges): escalate as
  **PRIORITY-SAFETY** immediately and advise the customer to stop using the product.
