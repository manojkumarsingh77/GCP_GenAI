# Cymbal Retail — Shipping & Delivery FAQ (v3.1, Effective Mar 2026)

## Delivery Options
| Option | Timeline | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | 4–7 business days | Free on orders over ₹999 / $25, else ₹79 / $3.99 |
| Express | 2–3 business days | ₹149 / $7.99 |
| Same-Day (select metros) | Order by 12 PM local time | ₹199 / $9.99 |
| Store Pickup | Ready in 2 hours | Free |

## Order Processing
- Orders placed before 3 PM local time ship the same business day.
- Orders are packed at the nearest regional fulfillment center with stock.
- Once shipped, customers receive a tracking link by SMS and email.

## Tracking Statuses Explained
- **PROCESSING** — payment confirmed, order being packed.
- **SHIPPED** — handed to the carrier; tracking number active.
- **IN_TRANSIT** — moving between carrier hubs.
- **OUT_FOR_DELIVERY** — on the delivery vehicle; arrives today.
- **DELIVERED** — delivery confirmed by carrier scan or photo.
- **DELAYED** — carrier reported an exception (weather, address issue, hub backlog).

## Delayed Shipments
- If a shipment shows DELAYED for more than **48 hours**, support agents should
  proactively offer: (a) a new ETA, and (b) a ₹150 / $5 goodwill voucher.
- If a package is 7+ days past the promised date, treat it as **lost in transit**:
  offer a free replacement or full refund. Escalate to the Logistics queue.

## Address Changes
- Address can be changed free of cost while status is PROCESSING.
- After SHIPPED, address changes go through the carrier and are not guaranteed.

## International Shipping
- Available to 42 countries. Duties and taxes are calculated at checkout (DDP).
- International orders cannot use Same-Day or Store Pickup.

## Failed Deliveries
- Carriers attempt delivery 3 times, then hold the package at the local hub for 5 days.
- Unclaimed packages return to the warehouse and are auto-refunded minus shipping.
